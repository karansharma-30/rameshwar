About Us

Know About Our Namkeens & Our History


Established in 1970, Shree Rameshwar has been a trusted name in the world of namkeen since its inception in Rajasthan, India. With a rich heritage spanning over five decades, we have perfected the art of crafting authentic and flavorful snacks that capture the essence of traditional Rajasthani cuisine.

Our journey began with a passion for preserving and sharing the time-honored recipes that have been cherished by generations. From humble beginnings, we have grown into a renowned brand synonymous with quality, taste, and innovation.

Each of our namkeen products is a testament to our commitment to excellence, crafted with care using the finest ingredients and time-tested techniques. Our dedication to quality and authenticity has earned us the trust and loyalty of customers both locally and around the globe.

Today, as we continue to expand our reach and offerings, we remain steadfast in our mission to delight palates and bring people together through the joy of great food. Join us on our journey as we invite you to experience the timeless flavors and unmatched quality of Shree Rameshwar namkeen.
